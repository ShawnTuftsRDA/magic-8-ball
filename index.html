Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Magic 8-Ball?
The Magic 8-Ball is a novelty toy invented in the 1950s. You ask a yes or no question, shake it, and a floating icosahedron (20-sided die) inside reveals one of 20 possible answers through a window.
How many answers does the Magic 8-Ball have?
The classic Magic 8-Ball has 20 responses: 10 positive, 5 neutral, and 5 negative. Our version includes all classics plus bonus Meme and Savage mode answers.
Is the Magic 8-Ball accurate?
The Magic 8-Ball selects responses randomly. It is a toy intended for entertainment only. Please do not make major life decisions based on its answers.
What is the best question to ask the Magic 8-Ball?
Yes/no questions work best. Avoid open-ended questions. Classic examples: "Will I get the job?", "Does my crush like me?", "Should I eat the last slice of pizza?"
